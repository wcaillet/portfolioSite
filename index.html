Hello, I am Wensie Caillet...

I'm a UX Developer with years of experience in development, product, project, and program management. Currently based in Houston, Texas. I have worked with startups to mid-sized companies, and have experience leading dev and design teams across numerous projects as a Product and Project Manager.
I got my start at the programming bootcamp The Iron Yard, then found my passion for design and completed a UX Design course with Bloc.io. After several years, I made the leap into management roles and have taken projects from ideation to launch.
Developing new products is not just a job for me, it's my passion.
